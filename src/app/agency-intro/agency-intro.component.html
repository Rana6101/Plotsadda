Key Features for Agencies

Unlock advanced capabilities the moment you create your agency account. Here’s what you get:

  1. Create Your Agency Profile

    Build a professional agency profile complete with logo, description, and contact details. Your agency will also appear in the PlotAdda.com directory for added exposure.

  2. Unlimited Property Listings

    Post, manage, and showcase unlimited residential, commercial, and plot listings. Every listing carries your agency’s name and branding, reinforcing your credibility in the marketplace.

  3. Upload, Edit & Delete Properties

    Stay in full control of your property portfolio—upload new properties, update details instantly, or remove outdated listings with a single click.

  4. Agency Logo Branding

    Attach your agency logo to every property you list so your brand identity stands out across Buy, Sell, and Commercial pages.

  5. Team Member Management

    As the agency owner, you can create team member accounts with individual logins, set secure passwords, and add or remove members at any time.

  6. Assign Roles & Designations

    Define clear roles within your agency by assigning team members specific designations (e.g., Sales Manager, Property Agent, Marketing Coordinator) to maintain a clear workflow and responsibilities.

  7. Agency Profile with Team Display

    Showcase your team members directly on your agency profile to build trust and credibility with potential clients.

  8. Agency Packages

    Choose from flexible packages designed for agencies of all sizes. Upgrade your plan anytime to access premium features like highlighted listings, extended team capacity, and advanced tools.

  9. Property Analytics

    Access detailed analytics on property views, customer inquiries, and listing engagement. Use this data to make smarter, data-driven decisions.

  10. Refresh Credits

    Re-promote listings to the top of search results whenever needed for maximum visibility.

  11. Free Banner Upload (Upcoming Feature)

    Soon, agencies will be able to upload promotional banners for free—another way to highlight your brand and promotions across the platform.
